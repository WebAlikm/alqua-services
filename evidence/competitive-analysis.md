# Competitive Analysis

This document compares AlQua'a Services with likely current alternatives.

## Alternatives

| Alternative | Strength | Limitation | Product Response |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| WhatsApp groups | Familiar and widely used | Information gets buried and is hard to search | Provide searchable listings and shareable links |
| Instagram posts | Good for discovery by active users | Not structured by need, urgency, or audience | Add filters and listing types |
| Government portals | Authoritative | Can be broad, formal, and hard for low-comfort users | Provide local, simplified entry points |
| Word of mouth | Trusted | Inconsistent and excludes people outside the network | Centralize verified community information |

## Positioning

AlQua'a Services is not trying to replace official portals or WhatsApp. It acts as a simple local layer that helps residents discover the right listing, then move to the right contact channel.
