# Notification Speed

Evidence for SC5: WhatsApp delivery or share flow completes in under 30 seconds.

## Test Method

Record timestamps for:

1. User taps WhatsApp/share action.
2. WhatsApp opens or message is received.
3. User can view the shared listing text/link.

## Results

| Test | Start Time | End Time | Duration | Result | Evidence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| WhatsApp share listing | Pending | Pending | Pending | Pending | Pending |

## Constraint

The hackathon prototype may use WhatsApp deep links or share intent. A full broadcast notification system is a future production extension unless time allows.
