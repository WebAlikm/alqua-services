# Challenge Context

## Selected Challenge

Challenge 4 - Connecting residents to services, opportunities and events.

## Local Context

Al Qua'a is a dispersed rural community in the Al Ain region. Information about useful services, events, and opportunities may move through informal channels, which can make discovery inconsistent for residents.

## Specific Problem

Residents do not always have a central, easy-to-use way to discover what is available nearby. This can cause people to miss time-sensitive opportunities, services, or events that could benefit them.

## Product Strategy

Build a narrow, working discovery hub that proves the core flow:

1. Resident opens the app on mobile.
2. Resident filters by need, audience, or urgency.
3. Resident finds a relevant listing.
4. Resident contacts the provider or opens map directions.

The project should score well by being clear, usable, feasible, and easy to verify from the repository.
